Software Development Lead
Position Overview We are looking for a highly skilled and motivated Software Development Lead to oversee our software development team both in development and application with business operation level. The successful candidate will be responsible for leading a team of talented developers, driving the development process, and ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality software products. As a Software Development Lead, you will play a key role in shaping the technical direction of our projects, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and fostering a culture of continuous improvement.
Key Responsibilities
  1. Team Building and Leadership:
    • Build and maintain software development team in cooperation with company functions
    • Lead and mentor the team of software developers, fostering a collaborative and innovative work environment.
    • Provide guidance and support team members, promoting growth and develop skill.
    • Collaborate with all departments to align development efforts with overall goals.
  2. Technical Leadership:
    • Review the existing apps & software and improve appropriately.
    • Design and analyze the database system and administration.
    • Tending the apps with continuous maintenance and update.
    • Define and drive the technical vision, strategy, and architecture for software.
    • Take technical decisions, ensure alignment with industry best practices and standards.
    • Conduct regular code reviews and provide constructive feedback for quality code.
  3. Project Management:
    • Plan, organize, and prioritize development tasks to meet project deadlines.
    • Work closely with project managers to define project scope, goals, and deliverables.
    • Track and report project progress, identifying and addressing potential risks.
  4. Collaboration and Communication:
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, design, and quality assurance, to ensure seamless project execution.
    • Communicate effectively with stakeholders, providing updates on project status and addressing any concerns.
Basic Skills
  • Object Oriented application development with leadership ability
  • Strong basic knowledge of software engineering and architecture
  • Quick learner of any new software technology
  • Committed to deliver the job's target and deadline with critical thinking ability
  • Motivated and team spirit in less supervision
Technical Skills
  • Knowledge on data structures, modeling, analysis, analytics and business structures
  • Web (ASP.NET Core/C#) and iOS (Swift)/Android (Kotlin) development skills
  • Latest web technologies and frameworks (HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery etc)
  • Skills and experience with data analysis, analytics and database technology (SQL queries)
  • Detailed knowledge of database systems (especially MySQL), Agile project management, testing tools
  • Strong knowledge of SQL including stored procedures and database development tooling
  • Good debugging and problem solving
  • Comprehend open-source projects.
  • Experience with cloud services especially Azure
  • Knowledge of video processing/based system operation.
