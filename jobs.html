Software Development Team Lead

Company Overview
Tlece Bangladesh Limited is fully starting this coming February 2024 in Bangladesh as a subsidiary of Global Ride, Inc., a Japan's corporation, and aggressively desires to operate its business with a very dynamic and innovative job matching, social networking, training and career development platforms to ease the recruitment and selection process for both employers and job seekers. Besides that, the company will also expand their business in IT & ITES based opportunities. So, the company is looking to fill this position with diverse capacity and ability to lead the system and apps development with a very capable workforce from multisectoral discipline.
Position Overview
We are looking for a highly skilled and motivated Software Development Lead to oversee our software development team both in development and application with business operation level. The successful candidate will be responsible for leading a team of talented developers, driving the development process, and ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality software products. As a Software Development Lead, you will play a key role in shaping the technical direction of our projects, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and fostering a culture of continuous improvement.
Key Responsibilities
  1. Team Building and Leadership:
    • Build and maintain software development team in cooperation with company functions
    • Lead and mentor the team of software developers, fostering a collaborative and innovative work environment.
    • Provide guidance and support team members, promoting growth and develop skill.
    • Collaborate with all departments to align development efforts with overall goals.
  2. Technical Leadership:
    • Review the existing apps & software and improve appropriately.
    • Design and analyze the database system and administration.
    • Tending the apps with continuous maintenance and update.
    • Define and drive the technical vision, strategy, and architecture for software.
    • Take technical decisions, ensure alignment with industry best practices and standards.
    • Conduct regular code reviews and provide constructive feedback for quality code.
  3. Project Management:
    • Plan, organize, and prioritize development tasks to meet project deadlines.
    • Work closely with project managers to define project scope, goals, and deliverables.
    • Track and report project progress, identifying and addressing potential risks.
  4. Collaboration and Communication:
    • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product management, design, and quality assurance, to ensure seamless project execution.
    • Communicate effectively with stakeholders, providing updates on project status and addressing any concerns.
Basic Skills
  • Object Oriented application development with leadership ability
  • Strong basic knowledge of software engineering and architecture
  • Quick learner of any new software technology
  • Committed to deliver the job's target and deadline with critical thinking ability
  • Motivated and team spirit in less supervision
Technical Skills
  • Knowledge on data structures, modeling, analysis, analytics and business structures
  • Web (ASP.NET Core/C#) and iOS (Swift)/Android (Kotlin) development skills
  • Latest web technologies and frameworks (HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery etc)
  • Skills and experience with data analysis, analytics and database technology (SQL queries)
  • Detailed knowledge of database systems (especially MySQL), Agile project management, testing tools
  • Strong knowledge of SQL including stored procedures and database development tooling
  • Good debugging and problem solving
  • Comprehend open-source projects.
  • Experience with cloud services especially Azure
  • Knowledge of video processing/based system operation.
Qualifications and Experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or a related field
  • At least 10 years of experience in programming, business analysis, database administration, information technology, database architecture where 3 years experience from similar business trends shall be given preference.
  • Relevant trainings
Information
  • Type of Employment: Full Time
  • Job Location: Dhaka, Bangladesh
  • Working Hours: 08 Hours 9 AM - 6 PM (1 Hour Lunch Break)
  • Date of Joining: Expected by 01 March 2024
Compensation and Benefit
  • Remuneration Package: Attractive remuneration to the best suitable candidate
  • Bonus and Allowance: 2 Basic Remuneration as bonus after completion and confirmation of 6 months service and other allowances on monthly basis
  • Weekly Holiday: Weekly 2 days (Friday and Saturday)
  • General/Public Holiday: As per company policy
  • Casual and Sick Leave: As per Bangladesh Labor Law and Company Policy
How to apply
Send your application along with CV and Whatsapp number by email with subject "Software Development Team Lead" to jobs-bd (at) tlece.com. In your CV, include any proofs of your software development's skills and experiences (Github, Stack Overflow links etc.) covering the whole software development lifecycle.

UI/UX Designer

Position Overview
We are seeking a UI/UX designer to be responsible to understand the user requirements and design usable interfaces, perform usability testing, and create prototypes to validate design concepts. The position will use the information to develop digital products like websites, mobile apps, and software.
Key Responsibilities
  1. Research and Develop:
    • Do research to understand the needs, motivations, and behavior of the users.
    • Analyze the industry types and features to find or develop innovative design.
  2. Design and Testing:
    • Create wireframes for the website, software and application for different platforms
    • Prototypes, and high-fidelity mock-ups to simulate the final product
    • Developing visual elements that are in line with brand identity
    • Crafting visual elements, layout, color schemes, and interactive elements.
    • Design for accessibility standards and visual effects.
    • Organizing usability tests and validating against business goals
  3. Maintenance and Improvement:
    • Assesses and optimizes the performance of new and existing features
    • Develops and maintains detailed user-interface specifications
    • Leads the design for new experiences and improvements of existing experiences
  4. Collaboration and Communication:
    • Aligning design objectives with products to meet business requirements.
    • Facilitating effective communication between various departments, such as marketing and engineering, to ensure a cohesive product strategy
  5. Graphics Design:
    • Design features and layouts for social media and professional networks
    • Minimum knowledge and experience on video editing and social media operation
Basic Skills
  • Fluency in best practices for web-based information architecture and design
  • Strong knowledge of usability principles and techniques
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and storytelling skills
  • Experience working in a collaborative multi-disciplinary team and working directly with developers for implementation of designs
  • Experience in a highly Agile/scrum environment
  • Ability to work in a startup environment
Technical Skills
  • Familiarity with HTML/CSS/JavaScript for web and mobile app’s UI/UX design
  • Ability to work both in a group and independently
  • Time administration
  • Portfolio of work in UI/UX design for mobile and web platforms
  • Excellent knowledge of Sketch, InVision, Adobe Creative Suite, Figma and other softwares
Qualifications and Experience
  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science or graphics or web & apps design or related field, or Diploma or Certificate on Professional training on UI/UX Design
  • At least 3-5 years of experience with proven achievement on project achievement
  • Relevant trainings
