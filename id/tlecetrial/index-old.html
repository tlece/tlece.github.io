APA ITU TLECE

Tlece adalah media sosial baru untuk rekrutmen yang berbasiskan video. Pengguna Tlece tidak hanya bisa mengekspresikan dirinya melalui video dan berinteraksi dengan pengguna lain, tapi juga melihat lowongan kerja dan melamar menggunakan video. Aplikasi Tlece tersedia di smartphone Android maupun iOS.

[Video Evaluation]

In order to avoid any inconvenience to the company that uploaded the video or took the video, we utilize our original rating system. “Tlece” will offer more jobs to highly rated partners.

★★★★★

4 videos exist and a Q&A with the hiring manager message is completed. The hiring position matches the video with company official website. The company exceeding 100 employees. The number of employees must exceed 100, and a company director must be participating in the video and mention the number of employees in the comments.

★★★★

4 video clips　with complete recruiter's message, the hiring position matches the video content, and company's official website is must.

★★★

4 video clips, and the Q&A session with the recruiter's message has been perfectly completed.

★★

4 video clips are filmed, or the contents of the four videos overlap.

★

4(four) video clips are not filmed, or the contents of the four videos overlap.
