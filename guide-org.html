This page shows an overview of Tlece mobile app as guide to new users.

Screens structure

Tlece mobile app consists of main tabs: Home, Account, Activities and Settings. Between Account and Activities tabs there is a "+" button for creating profile video. At the top there are top tabs, depending on which bottom tab is tapped.

1. Home screen

When Home tab is tapped, Home screen is displayed. Home screen is also displayed after initial start up of the app. At the top of the Home screen are these top tabs: People and Job.

1-1. People sub-screen

When People tab is tapped, People screen is displayed. This is the default screen shown at Home screen.

1-2 Job sub-screen

When Job tab is tapped, Job screen with 3 sub-tabs are displayed: Organizations, Vacancies and Interviews.
  • Organizations
  • Vacancies. Vacancies tab is selected by default at Job screen, and it shows Vacancies sub-screen. Vacancies sub-screen shows vacancies videos from organizations.
  • Interviews. Interviews tab shows Interviews sub-screen, which contains a list of interview invitations and schedules.

2. Account screen

When Account tab is tapped, Account screen is displayed. At the top of the Home screen are these top tabs: Profile, CV and Meetings. Meeting is only displayed when the logged in person is a staff of an organization.

2-1. Profile sub-screen

When Profile tab is tapped, Profile screen is displayed. This is the default screen shown at Account screen.

2-2. CV sub-screen

When CV tab is tapped, CV screen is displayed.

2-3. Meetings sub-screen

When Meetings tab is tapped, Meetings screen is displayed.


3. Activities screen

When Activities tab is tapped, Activities screen is displayed.


4. Settings screen

When Settings tab is tapped, Settings screen is displayed.


Basic operation

Tap on Home tab and see profile videos of other users at Home screen.

Tap on "+" button at the bottom and create your own profile video. Tap on Account tab and see or delete your profile video(s)

Tap on Home tab and then on Job top tab and see job vacancy videos. Tap on "i" button on the right of the screen to see some more detail of the vacancy. Tap on ">" button on the right of the screen to apply to the vacancy.

When your application is processed and you got invitation for an interview, tap on Home tab and then on Job top tab, and then tap on Interviews icon, to see the list of interview invitations and schedules.
