Tlece Trial

Thank you for your participation in the trial for Tlece, the social media app for searching your future career!

1 Participant registration

1-1. Register yourself as Tlece Trial participant via this form

2 Use Tlece

The following are the steps we would like you to do for Tlece Trial:

2-1. See this, to know what Tlece is, and install the app and find out how to use it

2-2. On "Settings" tab, select the language of the app which is also the language of the video you will record/upload. The subtitle will be added automatically in the language you chose here.

2-3. On "Profile" menu of "Account" tab, fill out your profile details such as name, sex dll, and then create a video profile to introduce yourself. This will be public to all users of Tlece app.

2-4. On "CV" menu of "Account" tab, create a video CV to be seen only by the organization whose job vacancy you applied.

2-5. On "Vacancies" submenu of "Job" menu of "Account" tab, browse job vacancy videos and apply to the one you like, by tapping on ">" button on lower right of the screen and recording/uploading your cover letter video.

2-6. You will receive an invitation to question video interview, which you can see on the calendar at "Interviews" screen on the date you applied.

2-7. Tap on the invitation and you can answer the question video by recording/uploading your answer video.

2-8. After registration to Tlece and uploading your profile video, after a while "FutureMe" menu on "Home" tab will be populated by profession videos with your face. Browse the videos and when you tap a video that you like it will be played full-screen, which you can share by to your social media accounts such Facebook, Instagram etc.

2-9. Go to Tlece page on Facebook and click on Like button.
